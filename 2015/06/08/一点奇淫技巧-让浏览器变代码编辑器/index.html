一点奇淫技巧-让浏览器变代码编辑器

C/C++

data:text/html, <style type=%22text/css%22>#e{position:absolute;top:0;right:0;bottom:0;left:0;}</style><div id=%22e%22></div><script src=%22http://d1n0x3qji82z53.cloudfront.net/src-min-noconflict/ace.js%22 type=%22text/javascript%22 charset=%22utf-8%22></script><script>var e=ace.edit(%22e%22);e.setTheme(%22ace/theme/monokai%22);e.getSession().setMode(%22ace/mode/c_cpp%22);</script>
